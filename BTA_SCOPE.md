# Scope (MVP)

Inputs:
- Teaching & Supervision
- Research & Publication
- Administration & Service
- Select pathway (Guru/Pensyarah/Penyelidik/Pentadbir/Perubatan)

Outputs:
- Total hours/week (target 40)
- Breakdown by component
- Warnings if minimum by pathway not met

Non-goals (MVP):
- Approval workflow
- User roles
- Database storage
